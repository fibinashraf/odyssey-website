About Us

Odyssey Transport & Delivery Services is a licensed transportation provider based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. We specialize in the coordination and delivery of light goods across the UAE with a strong commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction.

With a growing demand for fast and secure transport solutions, we established Odyssey to serve individuals and businesses who value efficiency, accountability, and affordable service. Whether you're a startup needing regular deliveries or a business seeking a dependable logistics partner, Odyssey is here to support your journey.

Our Services

  • Light Goods Transport: Transportation of non-heavy, small to medium-sized items across Abu Dhabi and throughout the UAE.
  • Delivery Coordination Services: We manage and organize delivery schedules for your goods, ensuring timely handovers and minimal delays.
  • Flexible Contracts: Whether you need one-time delivery or a recurring logistics solution, we offer contract-based options that fit your scale.
  • Business Support: Ideal for e-commerce, warehouse, food, and retail sectors looking for last-mile delivery solutions.
